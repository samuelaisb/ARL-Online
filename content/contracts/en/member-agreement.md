### **Membership Agreements for the Activist Resource Library**

*The Activist Resource Library (ARL) is a project of* Finance Engage Sustain *(FES) and* Apathy is Boring (AisB)*, graciously hosted at the Apathy is Boring’s head office in Montreal.* 

As a Member borrowing materials under the Activist Resource Library at AisB, the following are agreed to:

- Starting today, you will access the Activist Resource Library, a project by the Finance Engage Sustain (FES)’s The Youth Harbour and Apathy is Boring (AisB).

1. **Duration**
  1. This agreement is in effect indefinitely. Anything beyond that will need to be renewed.
2. **Termination**
  1. A minimum of 7 days notice is required by either party if termination of the membership occurs.
  2. FES and/or Apathy is Boring (AisB) have the right to terminate the agreement, and therefore your Activist Resource Library Membership, at any time if any policies or procedures are not followed by the Member and are deemed as negatively affecting other members of the Activist Resource Library or other members in the AisB building. There is zero tolerance for the violation of safety and respect of the space and other members by Members. Membership will be terminated immediately if this occurs.  
    

3. **Respectful and Supportive Community**
  1. The member commits to treating all staff members and fellow community members with kindness, respect, and understanding. Everyone's time, efforts, and perspectives are valued. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for harassment or discrimination of any kind. Everyone deserves to participate in a safe and welcoming environment.
  2. The Member commits to fostering a supportive and collaborative environment where everyone feels welcome and encouraged to participate. Our lending library thrives on mutual respect and collective responsibility.   
    
    

4. **Facility Responsibility and Care**
  1. It is understood that any physical damage of the AisB building, including the Activist Resource Library shelf, common spaces, office furniture, paint, holes in drywall, flooring, ceiling, lights, doors and glass, is the responsibility of the Member to repair. You may be required to pay for the damage that was caused by your negligence.  
  2. It is understood that prior to the end of the lease agreement the Member will ensure that the office space is left in suitable condition. This includes, but is not limited to, the repair of any damage to the space, and furniture removed, unless a prior agreement has been made to leave furniture.
  3. On termination and expiration of your Membership, you are responsible for returning to FES and AisB all of its property, including but not limited to any access cards or material/supply rentals.   
    

5. **Care and Accountability for Borrowed Materials**
  1. The Member commits to treating all borrowed materials and tools with care and respect. Items should be kept in good condition and returned in the same or better state as when they were borrowed.
  2. The Member agrees to take responsibility for their actions regarding borrowed items. If something goes wrong, the Member will work with the coordinator, FES, and AisB to find a solution, including repairing or replacing items if needed.
  3. FES and AisB have discretion to decide *if* you severely damage an item that they may need to pay an appropriate amount which is at our discretion.   
    

6. **Returning Items and Communication**
  1. The Member commits to returning all borrowed items by the agreed-upon due date. This helps ensure that everyone in the community has timely access to the materials they need.
  2. The Member agrees to communicate clearly and promptly with the library coordinator about any issues, questions, or changes in borrowing plans. If an item is damaged, lost, or cannot be returned on time, the Member will inform the Coordinator as soon as possible.  
    

7. **Feedback and Improvement**
  1. The Member agrees to provide constructive feedback to help improve the lending library and its operations. Suggestions for enhancing the borrowing experience or expanding the collection are always welcome.
  2. FES and AisB encourages sharing knowledge and skills related to the use of borrowed materials and tools. If you, the Member, have expertise, consider offering tips or guidance to fellow borrowers, contributing to a stronger and more knowledgeable community.
  3. Approaching the end of the one (1) year Membership, all Members will be required to complete a survey and impact reporting as outlined by FES and AisB.   
    

8. **Adherence**
  1. The Member agrees to adhere to the library’s borrowing procedures, including signing out items properly and providing necessary information to the Coordinator. This ensures that everyone has fair and organized access to the library’s resources.
  2. The Member agrees to adhere and align themselves and/or the organization to The Youth Harbour’ principles as outlined ++[here](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1MCSRSgpx_DVekRn8mozNPMsIRTwZ_8NV/view?usp=share_link)++ and Apathy is Boring’s principles as outlined ++[here](https://www.apathyisboring.com/about_us)++. 
  3. The Member agrees to adhere and align myself and/or the organization with what is outlined in the Membership Package and Agreement: Principles, Policies, and Procedures. 

These agreements help ensure that the lending library remains a resourceful, respectful, and well-maintained community space. In signing this, The Member agrees to respect the membership agreements of the Library. 



I have read, understood, and agree to adhere to all policies and procedures outlined in the Membership Agreement.
